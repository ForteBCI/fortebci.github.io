"Create music through thought alone"

Our Goal

For those who can no longer play, we bring the music back. Through the power of technology and the passion for sound, we give voice to silent hands and restore the joy of creating what the heart never forgot - music.

Our Device

This device allows users to create music in real-time through thought alone, offering a unique form of therapy for those with limited motor function. We’ve used EEG’s and machine learning to translate imagined musical pitch into music.

The Founders

At ForteBCI, we are a passionate team of engineers, neuroscientists, musicians, and creators united by one vision: to bring the power of music back to those who can no longer perform. Every member of our team carries a deep belief that music is more than sound—it’s identity, memory, and emotion. And we’re here to make it accessible again, through the intersection of technology and humanity.
